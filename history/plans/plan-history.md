# Plan History

Historical plans, remediation notes, decision packets, closure notes, and old
work sequencing records live here.

Use this folder for background context. Current plans and unresolved decisions
belong in `docs/planning/`; active execution state belongs in `.agents/state/`
and `.codex/context/`.

Typical files:
- historical `*-plan-*.md`
- closure notes
- remediation plans
- old decision packets
- superseded planning indexes
