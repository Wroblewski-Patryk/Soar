# Raw Artifacts

Raw generated artifacts live here: JSON, TXT, LOG, CSV, XLSX, screenshots, and
move manifests.

These files are evidence attachments, not narrative documentation. Prefer
linking to a human-readable record in `history/evidence/`, `history/audits/`,
or `history/releases/` when one exists.

Typical files:
- `_artifacts-*.json`
- generated logs and text outputs
- screenshots
- move manifests
