# Evidence History

Human-readable proof records live here: smoke checks, production readbacks,
restore and rollback proof, SLO observations, monitoring evidence, and other
verification reports.

Use this folder when you need to prove what was observed at a point in time.
Raw machine output belongs in `history/artifacts/`.

Typical files:
- smoke reports
- readback reports
- restore drill and rollback proof summaries
- SLO window reports
- deployment/build-info evidence
