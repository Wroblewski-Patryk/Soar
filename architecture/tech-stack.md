## Constraints

- Add new dependencies only when they reduce real complexity or match established project patterns.
- Security-sensitive dependencies require review and update notes.
- Major upgrades require planning, tests, rollback notes, and docs.
- Tooling choices must be automatable by agents and runnable locally.
