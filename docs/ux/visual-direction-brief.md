# Visual Direction Brief

## Product Character
- Brand feeling: technical sharpness with premium restraint
- Trust posture: serious, high-signal, operator-first
- Energy level: fast and confident, never casino-loud
- Keywords: control, signal, precision, risk awareness

## Visual Thesis
- Soar should feel like a modern operator cockpit for trading decisions, not a
  neon crypto theme or generic SaaS CRUD panel.

## Surface Notes
- Mobile: monitor and intervene quickly
- Tablet: supervise and review with touch comfort
- Desktop: dense, scan-friendly decision environment with persistent context

## Explicit Avoids
- gambling aesthetics
- noisy gradients and glow
- dense tables without hierarchy
