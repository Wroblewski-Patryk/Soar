# Experience Quality Bar

- Lead with decision clarity, system status, and risk awareness.
- Make primary operator actions obvious in the first viewport.
- Design loading, empty, error, and success states as operational moments.
- Mobile should prioritize alerting, monitoring, and fast intervention.
- Tablet should improve browsing and review comfort, not mirror desktop.
- Desktop should support scanning, comparison, and contextual decision making.
- Motion should clarify causality and feedback, never feel decorative.
- Fidelity to approved art direction matters when a canonical visual exists.
- Use real assets when texture or illustration matters and code would flatten
  the result.
