# Glossary

- Strategy. A set of rules and parameters that produce trade signals.
- Signal. A recommendation to enter or exit based on strategy rules.
- SymbolGroup. A named set of tradable symbols reusable in bot bindings.
- BotStrategy. A runtime binding of Bot + SymbolGroup + Strategy.
- Position. An open trade with defined risk controls.
- Backtest. Simulation of a strategy on historical data.
- AI Assistant. A user-scoped AI agent with its own mandate and risk/autonomy settings.
- TP. Take profit level.
- SL. Stop loss level.
- Trailing. Dynamic adjustment of stop based on price movement.
